I'm a postdoctoral researcher in the Information and Communication Theory (ICT) Lab at the Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e).

Biography

Yunus Can Gültekin received the B.Sc. and the M.Sc. degrees from Middle East Technical University, Ankara, Turkey, both in electrical and electronics Engineering, in 2013 and 2015, respectively. He obtained his Ph.D. degree in 2020 from TU/e, the Netherlands.
